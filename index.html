Visit Aburi

Enjoy culture and nature in this vibrant district at the eastern of Ghana

Top three places to visit in Aburi

Visit the beautiful Umbrella Rock

The rock has an overhang large enough for 12 to 15 people at once.

Visit the Abetifi Stone Age Park

An archaeological discovery of caves with 12,500 year old artifacts.

Enjoy hiking and the view at Atewa Range

The range consists of steep-sided hills with fairly flat summits.
